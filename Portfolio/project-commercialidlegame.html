Idle Fields

This game is my first indie commercial title. The game is being developed in Unity and is released on Steam February 20th 2024.

What Is The Game?

Idle Fields is a 2D cute idle skilling game where players level up skills. Each skill comes with its own gameplay

Idle Fields have 8 skills to offer:

  • Farming
  • Fishing
  • Cooking
  • Husbandry
  • Woodcutting
  • Construction
  • Mining
  • Smithing

These skills can be levelled up to 50 (with virtual levels shown once you reach max). The player can farm, collect produce from animals, fish, cook various recipes, cut trees, mine, smith, and decorate your own home.

Idle Fields has been developed by myself, Joshua Mobley, and Mark Wolff. The game has also been tested by my lovely community on Discord.

Currently, the game is still being worked on with content updates coming out

This game is being developed as part of a devlog series on YouTube. This means that I produce video updates where I showcase what is new for the game. Not only do I enjoy creating videos on the side but it also works as a form of advertisement for the game.
