Idle Fields

This game is my first indie commercial title that is currently work in progress. The game is being developed in Unity 2023 and is planned to be released on Steam early 2024.

What Is The Game?

Idle Fields is a 2D cute idle skilling game where players level up skills. Each skill comes with its own gameplay

For release, there are 6 skills

  • Farming
  • Fishing
  • Cooking
  • Husbandry
  • Woodcutting
  • Construction

These skills can be levelled up to 50 (with virtual levels shown once you reach max). The player can farm, collect produce from animals, fish, cook various recipes, cut trees, and decorate your own home.

I plan on creating a blog on the outcome of the release once the game goes live. This page will also be updated accordingly.

This game is being developed as part of a devlog series on YouTube. This means that I produce video updates where I showcase what is new for the game. Not only do I enjoy creating videos on the side but it also works as a form of advertisement for the game.
